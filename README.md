ðŸ“ˆ Key Analysis & Insights
1. The Conversion Funnel
I built a 3-stage funnel to track the customer journey:

Stage 1: Total Contacts (Initial reach out)

Stage 2: Interested Leads (Filtered by call duration > 120 seconds)

Stage 3: Successful Subscriptions (Final conversion)

Result: The overall campaign achieved a 11.70% conversion rate.

2. Behavioral Drivers (Duration)
Insight: There is a direct correlation between call duration and success. Customers who subscribed spent significantly more time on the call compared to those who refused.

3. Demographic Targeting (Job & Education)
Insight: Management, Technicians, and Retired individuals showed the highest interest. Targeting these specific segments can reduce marketing costs and improve ROI.
